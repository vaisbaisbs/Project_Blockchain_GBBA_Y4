Distribution of social attention within the portfolio

This graph illustrates the popularity of cryptocurrencies on Reddit, a social media platform organized into communities where users discuss and share opinions about various topics, including cryptocurrencies. As such, Reddit activity can be used as a proxy for public attention, investor sentiment and the level of interest surrounding each asset. You can watch this video to learn more about Reddit social media on cryptocurrencies:

A clear hierarchy emerges from the data. Bitcoin dominates significantly, with the highest score and the largest bubble, indicating very strong visibility and discussion volume. Ethereum follows, also showing substantial community engagement. Dogecoin displays moderate popularity, while the other cryptocurrencies exhibit relatively low levels of Reddit activity, suggesting more limited visibility within online communities.

Portfolio Strategy

Based on the previous analysis, the portfolio should maintain a balanced exposure between highly visible assets and those driven by stronger fundamentals. Bitcoin and Ethereum should remain core holdings, as they benefit from both strong fundamentals and high levels of public attention, which can support sustained investor interest. Assets characterized by strong community-driven popularity, such as Dogecoin, should be approached with caution. While they may offer short-term opportunities, they also carry higher volatility and speculative risk and should represent a limited portion of the portfolio. For less visible cryptocurrencies, their allocation should be based on their underlying fundamentals rather than their level of popularity. If supported by strong use cases or development activity, they may still offer long-term value despite lower current attention.

Conclusion

This graph highlights the unequal distribution of social attention across cryptocurrencies, with a strong concentration on a few dominant assets. While popularity can support visibility and short-term momentum, it does not necessarily guarantee long-term value. Social metrics such as Reddit activity should therefore be interpreted as indicators of market sentiment and used alongside fundamental and on-chain analysis when evaluating a portfolio.
