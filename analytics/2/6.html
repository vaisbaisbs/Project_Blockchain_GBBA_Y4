What is a GitHub Activity ?

GitHub activity refers to the level of development taking place on a blockchain project. It is typically measured through indicators such as commits, forks, stars and issues on GitHub repositories. This metric is essential because it reflects the technical effort behind a cryptocurrency, showing whether the project is actively maintained and evolving. Unlike price, it provides insight into the long-term innovation and sustainability of a blockchain.

Distribution of development activity within the Portfolio

The graph displays a GitHub activity score out of 100 for each cryptocurrency in the portfolio. Bitcoin clearly dominates, reaching the maximum score of 100, indicating a very strong and active development ecosystem. Ethereum follows with a score of around 56, which also reflects significant and sustained development activity. A second group, including XRP, Solana and Dogecoin, shows moderate levels of activity, with scores between roughly 19 and 24. In contrast, the other cryptocurrencies exhibit relatively low development activity. Overall, the graph highlights a strong concentration of development activity within a few major projects, whereas the rest of the portfolio shows significantly lower levels of innovation.

Recommendations

From a portfolio perspective, priority should be given to assets with strong and sustained development activity, as they are more likely to adapt and remain competitive over time. Bitcoin and Ethereum should remain core holdings, given their active developer ecosystems and continuous improvement. Their strong technical foundation supports long-term resilience. Assets with moderate activity such as Solana or XRP should be monitored, as their future potential depends on their ability to maintain or increase development efforts.

Conclusion

The analysis of GitHub activity highlights significant differences in the level of innovation across the portfolio. Some assets benefit from strong and continuous development, like Bitcoin and Ethereum. However, others show limited activity, which may affect their long-term competitiveness.
